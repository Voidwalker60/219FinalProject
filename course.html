9-Hole Course

Our 9-Hole course is perfect for those looking for a short and enjoyable round of golf. With shorter fairways and a welcoming layout, this course is ideal for beginners, families, and golfers who prefer a faster game. The peaceful setting, highlighted by the rolling Okanagan countryside, makes for a relaxing experience while offering a great opportunity to improve your golf skills without the pressure of a full 18-hole round.
