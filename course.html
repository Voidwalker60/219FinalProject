Courses we have offer!

Choosing between our scenic 9-Hole and full 18-Hole experiences.

9-Hole course

Our 9-Hole course is perfect for those looking toward a short and enjoyable round of golf. With short fairways and a welcoming layout, this course is ideal for beginners, families and golfers who prefer a faster game. The environment is peaceful,with low rolling countryside the walking is made easy which is ideal for improving your skills without the pressure of a full 18-hole round.

18-Hole Championship Course

The 18-Hole course has a fuller range of golfing facilities and services including all skill levels. The course has wide fairways, changes in elevation, and strategic hazards making it enjoyalble and exciting for every golfer. The full-length length layout is popular with regular players due to the strategy and skill it requires.
