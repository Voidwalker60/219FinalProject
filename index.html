Kelowna's premier golf club

Featuring world-class courses and excellent staff, the Okanagan Golf and country club offers its members an exemplary course and excellent dining.

Experience the pinnacle of Okanagan golfing across our two distinct championship courses. Challenge yourself on 'The Ridge,' designed to test your strategy with dramatic elevation changes, or enjoy the scenic beauty of 'The Valley,' a meticulously manicured parkland layout. After your round, join us at The Last Putt, our signature restaurant serving locally sourced cuisine and creating the perfect atmosphere to celebrate your game. Interested in joining our community? Visit our Memberships page to explore our exclusive privileges and packages.
