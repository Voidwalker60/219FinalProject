The Last putt restaurant

Chilliwack beef, Armstrong cheese, and exquisite salmon caught off the coast of Vancouver island are a staple of our diverse menu at the Last Putt.

At The Last Putt, we believe that exceptional dining begins with the finest local ingredients. Our culinary team is proud to showcase the best of British Columbia, crafting a diverse menu that features tender Chilliwack beef, rich Armstrong cheese, and exquisite salmon caught fresh off the coast of Vancouver Island. Whether you are celebrating a hole-in-one or simply enjoying an evening out, join us for a meal that captures the true taste of the region.
