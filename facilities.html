Okanagan Country Club Gymnasium

The Okanagan Golf and Country Club has a modern and well-stocked gym for all your fitness needs

Elevate your game off the course at our state-of-the-art fitness center. Featuring top-tier cardio and strength-training equipment, our gymnasium is designed with the golfer in mind, offering dedicated spaces for flexibility training and core conditioning to prevent injury and improve your swing. With floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the 18th green, you can find inspiration in the view while you train. Personal trainers are also available to craft custom fitness plans to help you add yards to your drive and stamina to your game.

Pro Shop

Our fully stocked Pro Shop offers the latest in golf equipment and apparel from top brands. Whether you need a club fitting, new soft-spikes, or a souvenir polo, our knowledgeable professionals are here to assist you with expert advice to improve your game.

Practice Facilities

Perfect your swing at our expansive driving range, featuring grass tees and target greens. Sharpen your short game at our dedicated chipping area with sand bunkers and two large putting greens that mirror the speed of our course.
